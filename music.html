Approach

Why pursue both?

Chamber music demands the same analytical attention as antenna design — phase relationships, balance, timing, the discipline of listening for what's actually there versus what you expect. Programs with both serious EE research and a robust chamber music ecosystem (Michigan, UT Austin, CU Boulder, Ohio State) are where I'd be most at home.
