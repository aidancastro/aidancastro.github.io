Compact Dual-Band PIFA for LTE Bands 3 & 8

HFSS · RO4350B substrate · 35×10×5 mm bounding volume

Designed a meandered, folded planar inverted-F antenna covering LTE Band 8 (880–960 MHz) and LTE Band 3 (1.71–1.88 GHz) within a 35×10×5 mm volume on a 120×60 mm ground plane. A shorted parasitic strip recovers the LTE 3 bandwidth lost to the compact footprint. The design is benchmarked against the Chu–McLean Q bound (ka ≈ 0.35) to characterize the size–bandwidth tradeoff.

mmWave FMCW MIMO Radar Imaging

MATLAB · IMAGEVK-74 · 20×20 virtual array · 3D backprojection

Built a 3D backprojection imaging pipeline for an FMCW MIMO module scanned in a snake pattern via a modified 3D-printer stage. Implemented FOV masking, coherent vs. incoherent summation, background subtraction, outlier rejection for corrupted TX/RX pairs, and near-field bistatic phase calibration using redundant-baseline techniques. Currently extending toward MIMO-SAR imaging with a literature survey contrasting commercial mmWave modules against custom-built systems.
