About

Two disciplines, one habit of mind.

I'm an electrical engineering student at the University of South Florida working on RF/microwave circuit design, antenna simulation, and radar imaging. In parallel, I perform chamber music as a violist — most recently a solo recital featuring Bach, Bloch, Clarke, and Beach.

What ties the two together is a fascination with how structured signals — electromagnetic or acoustic — carry information through space. My research interests sit at the intersection of antenna systems, radar signal processing, and machine learning for RF. My musical work centers on chamber repertoire that demands the same kind of analytical listening.
