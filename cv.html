Research & Projects

2025 – present

Dual-Band PIFA — LTE 3 / LTE 8

EEL 4461/5462 · Dr. Mumcu · HFSS

Compact meandered PIFA on RO4350B in a 35×10×5 mm volume. Parasitic strip for LTE-3 bandwidth recovery. Benchmarked against Chu–McLean Q bound.

2024 – 2025

mmWave FMCW MIMO Radar Imaging

Independent research · MATLAB · IMAGEVK-74

3D backprojection pipeline across a 20×20 virtual array. Near-field bistatic calibration via redundant baselines, outlier rejection, FOV masking. Extending toward MIMO-SAR.

2025

1.9 GHz LNA — BFP420 on FR4

RF/Microwave II · Group 2 · ADS

Two-stage matching with Modelithics models. Target: 14 ± 1.5 dB gain, NF ≤ 2.0 dB, unconditional stability. Layout-level cosimulation.

2025

900 MHz LNA — NXP PBR941

RF/Microwave II · ADS · Modelithics

Voltage-feedback bias network, DC feed/block selection, stability and NF analysis. Groupmate's design proceeded to fabrication.

2025

Rectangular Microstrip Patch — 4.2 GHz

EEL 4461/5462 · MATLAB + HFSS

End-to-end design from analytical parameter calculation through HFSS simulation with coaxial-probe feed.

2025

8-Element Linear Array — 2.4 GHz

EEL 4461/5462 · HFSS
